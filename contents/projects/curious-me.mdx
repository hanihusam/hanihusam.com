## Short Explanation

Curious Me is an anonymous social platform that lets people share thoughts and
connect with others nearby. This is duplicated content used to exercise the
works list, filtering, and load-more behaviour.

## What I Worked With

A React + TypeScript frontend styled with Tailwind CSS, with a component test
suite to keep the UI regressions in check.
